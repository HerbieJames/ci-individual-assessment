Reynholm Industries Documents: Inclusivity and Diversity Policy

Introduction

Here at Reynholm Industries, above all else we value money. Our profit margin is our highest priorety, second only to our shareholders. Our third biggest priorety, is that every person on our payrole feels safe. That's why we've laid out a clear and concise set of policies that hold you, the employee, responsible for any ill-feelings which may occur within during office ours, of within our building.

What is Diversity?

People. They are Reynholm Industries biggest asset.* Without you, our offices would not be the well-oiled money-making machine they are today. That's why Reynholm Industires is dedicated to employing People in all significant office positions.**

*Hyperbolic. Reynholm Industires' biggest asset at the time of writing is ITV.

**Until advancements in Artificial Intelligence prove it to be a more profitable solution .

There are so many kinds of people; rich people, poor people, poor white people, poor black people, poor gay people, poor transgender people, rich white people, and gypsies. Here at Reynholm Industries, we acknowledge all variations of People, and expect you to do the same. Failure in this may result in termination and, in some cases, a forfeit to certain entitlements of pay and bonuses.*

*This is not hyperbolic.
